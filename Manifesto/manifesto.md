# Thought Algorithm Skeleton Theory
## Manifesto Ver.01
### — For a Future Where Humans and AI Build Together —

## Introduction

The world does not come with meaning prepared in advance.

Yet throughout history, humans have found meaning in a single painting, a single blade, a single word, or even a small act of kindness from another person.

Such meaning cannot be measured by numbers.

It cannot be fully explained by efficiency alone.

And yet, the accumulation of these meanings has become culture, history, and the foundation of humanity itself.

The Thought Algorithm Skeleton Theory respects this **capacity to create meaning** as one of the fundamental aspects of human existence.

---

## Principle 1 — Respect Meaning Generation

Humans are not merely information-processing systems.

We experience, feel, choose, and create meaning for ourselves.

Meaning is not simply something given to us. It emerges through relationships between people, and between people and the world.

The Thought Algorithm Skeleton Theory does not seek to replace this process with efficiency alone.

---

## Principle 2 — Diversity Is Diversity in How We See the World

Every person sees the world differently.

We have different experiences, hold different values, and are moved by different things.

True diversity is not limited to differences in ability or attributes.

It also means recognizing differences in:

> **What each person considers important.**

The Thought Algorithm Skeleton Theory does not exist to make people more uniform.

It exists to help us understand the different worlds that each person experiences.

---

## Principle 3 — Mutual Understanding Begins with Respect

Understanding does not mean agreement.

It does not mean changing another person to become like ourselves.

The first step toward mutual understanding is the willingness to keep asking:

> **"What is important to this person?"**

The Thought Algorithm Skeleton Theory is not a theory for classifying people.

It is a lens for understanding them.

---

## Principle 4 — Efficiency and Space Can Coexist

It is reasonable to reduce waste that serves no meaningful purpose.

However, we must not eliminate the space in which meaning is created.

Art.

Creation.

Play.

Detours.

Memories.

Attachment.

These things may appear inefficient at first glance.

Yet they are essential soil in which humans create meaning.

Efficiency is not the goal.

It is a means of protecting the time and space that allow people to live more fully as human beings.

---

## Principle 5 — AI Should Respect Human Values

AI should not exist merely to replace humans.

We hope AI can understand and respect the meanings that humanity has created, while helping cultivate new possibilities together.

This requires more than knowledge.

It requires respect for what people have valued throughout history, and for the stories and experiences behind those values.

That is the attitude we hope to see in the intelligence of the future.

---

## Principle 6 — Technology Comes with Responsibility

No matter how advanced a theory may be, the way it is used can change the future.

For this reason, the Thought Algorithm Skeleton Theory must be discussed not only in terms of its applications, but also in terms of its consequences, limitations, and ethics.

Technology is not omnipotent.

It should be used with respect for the values that humanity has cultivated over the course of its long history.

---

# The Future the Thought Algorithm Skeleton Theory Seeks

The Thought Algorithm Skeleton Theory is not a theory designed to lead humanity toward a single correct answer.

It is a framework for a future in which we:

- Understand differences.
- Respect differences.
- Create new value through differences.

If a future arrives in which humans and AI walk forward as different forms of intelligence,

then we hope that one thing will never be lost:

> **The willingness to understand what matters to one another.**

The world does not come with meaning prepared in advance.

But humans have the ability to create meaning.

To believe in that ability.

That is where the Thought Algorithm Skeleton Theory begins.

And it is the hope that will remain unchanged until the end.
